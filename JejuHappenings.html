Jeju Happenings

Jeju Island offers a variety of exciting events throughout the year, each
highlighting the island's unique culture and heritage. Whether you're
looking to enjoy the island's natural beauty, experience its traditional
music and dance, or indulge in its delicious cuisine, there is something
for everyone on Jeju Island. Don't miss the chance to experience the
best of Jeju Island at these upcoming events.

Event of the Month

Bunker de Lumières: Monet, Renoir and Chagall

AMIEX, formerly an industrial site, has been transformed into a vibrant cultural space dedicated to showcasing the immersive media art of France. Get ready to be dazzled by the stunning exhibition that awaits you!
